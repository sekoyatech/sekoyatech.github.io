Building a mobile or web application today demands more than code — it requires a deep understanding of user behavior, platform conventions, and scalable architecture. At Sekoya, we combine product thinking with engineering precision to deliver applications that feel native on every device, load fast on every network, and scale as your user base grows.

Our team specializes in Flutter for cross-platform mobile development, enabling a single codebase to target iOS, Android, and the web without compromising on performance or visual fidelity. On the web side, we leverage modern frameworks such as React, Next.js, and Astro to build everything from high-traffic marketing sites to complex SaaS dashboards.

Every project follows a structured lifecycle: discovery workshops to clarify scope, interactive prototyping for rapid feedback, iterative sprints with continuous delivery, and post-launch monitoring to ensure production health. Whether you need an MVP to validate a market hypothesis or a full-featured platform serving millions, we have the tooling and the team to get you there.
