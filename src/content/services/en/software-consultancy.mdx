Making the wrong technology decision can cost months of development time and significant budget. Our consultancy practice helps organizations navigate the constantly shifting landscape of languages, frameworks, cloud providers, and tooling — so you invest in the right foundation from day one.

Sekoya consultants bring hands-on engineering experience, not just slide decks. We embed with your team to understand your codebase, infrastructure, and delivery pipeline before making recommendations. Whether you need an architecture review for an aging monolith, guidance on migrating to the cloud, or a pragmatic plan to reduce technical debt without halting feature delivery, we provide actionable advice grounded in real-world constraints.

Our engagements range from focused two-week assessments to ongoing advisory retainers. In every case, the output is a clear, prioritized roadmap your team can execute on — complete with trade-off analysis, effort estimates, and risk mitigation strategies.
