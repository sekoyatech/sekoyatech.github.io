The Internet of Things is transforming how businesses collect data, monitor assets, and automate operations — but connecting hardware to the cloud reliably at scale is a non-trivial engineering challenge. Sekoya delivers end-to-end IoT solutions that cover the entire stack: from sensor selection and firmware development through edge gateways, cloud ingestion pipelines, and real-time visualization.

We have deep experience with industrial IoT scenarios, including factory floor monitoring, predictive maintenance, and environmental sensing. Our solutions use battle-tested protocols like MQTT and CoAP, and we design every system with intermittent connectivity in mind — because real-world networks are never as stable as a lab environment. Data is processed at the edge where latency matters, and aggregated in the cloud where historical analysis and machine learning models add long-term value.

Security is embedded at every layer: mutual TLS for device-to-cloud communication, certificate-based device identity, encrypted storage, and role-based access for dashboards and APIs. Whether you are connecting ten sensors or ten thousand, our platform-agnostic approach ensures you are never locked into a single vendor.
