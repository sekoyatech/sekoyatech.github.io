Reliable software requires reliable infrastructure. Sekoya's DevOps and cloud engineering team builds the automation, observability, and deployment pipelines that let your developers ship with confidence — multiple times a day if needed — without sacrificing stability or security.

We design CI/CD workflows tailored to your stack and team size, from simple GitHub Actions pipelines for small teams to multi-stage, multi-environment pipelines with automated testing gates, security scanning, and canary deployments for larger organizations. Infrastructure is defined as code using Terraform or Pulumi, ensuring that environments are reproducible, auditable, and version-controlled alongside application code.

On the cloud side, we architect solutions across AWS, Google Cloud, and Azure — or hybrid and multi-cloud setups when the business requires it. Our designs prioritize cost efficiency from day one: right-sized instances, auto-scaling policies, reserved capacity planning, and regular FinOps reviews to eliminate waste. Combined with comprehensive monitoring through tools like Grafana, Prometheus, and Datadog, we ensure you have full visibility into system health and the confidence to respond quickly when something goes wrong.
