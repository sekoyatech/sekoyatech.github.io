Artificial intelligence is reshaping how organizations operate, communicate, and make decisions. Sekoya helps businesses harness the latest advances in AI — from large language models and generative AI to intelligent automation — in ways that are practical, secure, and aligned with real business goals rather than hype cycles.

Our AI practice covers a broad range of capabilities. We build Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems that ground language model responses in your proprietary data, reducing hallucinations and ensuring factual accuracy. We design conversational agents that handle customer inquiries, internal knowledge retrieval, and multi-step workflows with natural language interfaces. And we integrate AI into existing pipelines for tasks like document classification, entity extraction, and content generation at scale.

Every AI solution we deliver comes with guardrails. We implement structured evaluation frameworks, human-in-the-loop review stages where appropriate, and monitoring for output quality and fairness. Our approach is pragmatic: if a rule-based system outperforms a neural network for a given task, we will recommend the simpler solution. The goal is measurable value, not technology for its own sake.
