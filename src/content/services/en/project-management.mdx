A great codebase means nothing if the project delivering it is out of control. Sekoya provides experienced project managers who understand both the technical and business dimensions of software delivery, ensuring that timelines are realistic, scope is managed, and stakeholders stay informed throughout the process.

We apply Agile methodologies adapted to each project's context — not cookie-cutter Scrum ceremonies bolted onto a waterfall mindset. Our PMs facilitate sprint planning, backlog refinement, and daily coordination while maintaining a clear view of the bigger picture: budget burn rate, dependency mapping, and milestone health. When risks surface, we flag them early and present options rather than surprises.

For organizations building internal engineering capability, we also offer fractional PM support and coaching to help your own teams adopt sustainable delivery practices. The goal is always the same: predictable delivery of high-quality software that meets the needs of the people who will use it.
