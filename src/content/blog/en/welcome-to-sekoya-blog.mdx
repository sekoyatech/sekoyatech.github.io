We are excited to launch the official Sekoya blog. This is where our team will share technical deep-dives, lessons learned from real-world projects, and our perspective on the tools and practices that drive effective software engineering.

## What to Expect

Our content will span several areas that reflect the breadth of work we do at Sekoya:

- **Engineering deep-dives** — detailed posts on architecture decisions, framework comparisons, and implementation patterns we use in production.
- **Project retrospectives** — honest reflections on what worked, what didn't, and what we would do differently in past engagements.
- **Technology trends** — analysis of emerging tools, frameworks, and platforms, grounded in practical experience rather than speculation.
- **Team and culture** — insights into how we work, hire, and build a sustainable engineering culture.

## Why We Write

Writing forces clarity. When we document a solution or articulate a technical decision, we sharpen our own understanding and create a resource that helps others facing similar challenges. We believe the best engineering teams are ones that share knowledge openly — both within the organization and with the broader community.

## Stay Connected

New posts will be published regularly. You can subscribe via RSS or follow us on social media to get notified when new content goes live. If there is a topic you would like us to cover, feel free to reach out — we are always looking for questions worth answering.

Thank you for reading, and welcome aboard.
